What is Hyrost?

Hyrost is a sprawling RPG that marries the boundless creativity of a sandbox with the rich narrative of an epic adventure. Traverse a procedurally-generated realm, unearthing ancient dungeons, forgotten secrets, and confronting mythical beasts. Shape the very earth beneath your feet, block by block.

Our Early Access invites you to experience our polished core mechanics - from visceral, responsive combat to a robust and intuitive building system. We provide creator-first tools, empowering you to forge your own content, design challenging minigames, and govern your own community servers.

Enter the world of Hyrost, leave your indelible mark, and join us in shaping this ever-growing universe!
